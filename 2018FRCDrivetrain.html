2018 FRC Robot Drivetrain (Subteam Leader)

My Contributions:
The objective was to design, prototype, and fabricate a chassis and drive system within the constraints of the competition rules and the time frame set by the team. The chassis consisted of a custom-built frame made of 2”x1” aluminum stock and custom machined aluminum gussets. The drive system was powered by 6 Mini CIM electric motors powering a gearbox containing a custom gear train. The motors powered all 6 wheels of the tank drive (2 via direct drive and 4 via chain drive). With the exceptions of the gearbox plates and wheels themself, I did all the design work myself in Solidworks. I led the fabrication of the chassis and drive system and led two other students who aided me.

Skills Used:
Solidworks, technical & organizational leadership, metal fabrication, power tool use, design
